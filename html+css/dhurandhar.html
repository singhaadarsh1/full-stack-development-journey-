Hamza Ali Mazari is a fictional character played by Ranveer Singh in the 2026 Hindi film Dhurandhar: The Revenge. He is portrayed as an undercover Indian operative (RAW agent) whose real identity is Jaskirat Singh Rangi, infiltrating the Karachi criminal underworld, specifically the Lyari district, to dismantle a terror network

Jameel Jamali is a popular fictional character portrayed by actor Rakesh Bedi in the film Dhurandhar: The Revenge, inspired by controversial Pakistani politician Nabil Gabol from Lyari. The character is depicted as a powerful, often chaotic political figure with connections to ISI, crime, and intelligence in Pakistan, drawing immense attention for its comedic yet intense portrayal of Karachi
